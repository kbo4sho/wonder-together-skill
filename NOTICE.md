# Notice

Wonder Together is a parent-mediated learning packet system for adults and creators. It is not medical, legal, therapeutic, emergency, or child-supervision advice.

The included sample images are demo artifacts generated for visual review. They are intended to demonstrate the original Wonder Together visual direction: warm parent-child scenes, clear diagrams, soft field-guide colors, and simple hand-drawn linework.

Do not use this project to copy another artist's character, blob mascot, illustration identity, or proprietary visual style. The included `Big Wonderer` and `Little Wonderer` system is meant to remain diagram-supportive, gender-neutral, and original to Wonder Together.
